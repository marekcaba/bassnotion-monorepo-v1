> **v6.0 Strategy Note:** This specification describes the full Practice Bridge platform vision. The build order follows a Bassicology-first strategy: Bassicology (bass-specific education product) ships at Week 12-14, generating revenue immediately. Practice Bridge Desktop ships as a listener-first tray icon at Week 20-26. Practice Bridge is extracted as a standalone multi-instrument platform at Week 34-42. See Section 19 of [practice-bridge-business.md](practice-bridge-business.md) for the complete milestone timeline.
