## BASSICOLOGY BRIDGE (Desktop Plugin)

Lightweight desktop plugin connecting player's audio interface to platform for latency-free capture and precision analysis.

### Bridge Metrics (per track/exercise)
Timing accuracy, note accuracy, consistency (ms), speed without accuracy loss (BPM), groove/feel score, weakest section identification, before/after comparison.

### Shipping Order
- Products ship WITHOUT Bridge first
- **Bridge MVP (Month 4-5):** Capture audio, compare timing to MIDI, accuracy %, pack score. 4 metrics.
- **Bridge V2 (Month 8-12):** Note-by-note analysis, historical graphs, weakness detection, automated recommendations, real-time visual feedback.

### Bridge Impact
- Play-Along Packs become diagnostic tools with scoring
- Practice Packs show measurable improvement (BPM increase as fact, not feeling)
- Groove Finder becomes real playing test (5 exercises, 3 min)
- Groove Cards show real metrics (infinitely more shareable)
- Feedback reviewers focus on subjective only (tone/feel/musicality) → hiring threshold pushed from 100 to 250-300 members

## 8 RULES

1. **Proof before promises** - each phase creates evidence for the next
2. **Product sells the next product** - player feels the gap naturally
3. **Never present lower tiers during higher-tier pitch** - sequential exposure only
4. **Rollover credits push UP** - loss aversion sells
5. **Webinar teaches architecture** - what no product teaches
6. **Time-gate downsells** - $249 at day 5-7, $49/$69 at day 12-14
7. **Track revenue per lead** ($199 target), not conversion rate
8. **Simple scales, fancy fails** - 10 products, 3 clusters, 1 anchor, 1 decoy, 1 trial, 1 retention, 1 webinar
